FlyteX

The aviation industry has witnessed a significant increase in the number of travelers over the years, leading to a surge in demand for efficient flight booking platforms. Traditional flight booking systems often lack personalized recommendations and real-time pricing information, resulting in suboptimal choices for users. In response to this, the proposed project aims to develop FlyteX, a Flight Booking Web Application that utilizes predictive algorithms to recommend flights based on user preferences and predicts future prices for enhanced decision-making.
